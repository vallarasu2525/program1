About the College

Government Arts College, Nandanam, Chennai:600 035, originated in the mid-18th century as a school Muslim University called Madrasa. In 1901, it was upgraded to a college with the objective of accommodating students. This continued until it acquired its present status in 1948.

The college is affiliated with the University of Madras and offers various undergraduate and postgraduate courses in arts, commerce, and science streams. It has been recognized by the Grants Commission (UGC) and has a rich history of academic excellence.
